FAQ

Quick answers before we build your lead system.

Most local businesses do not need more random traffic. They need a clearer offer, faster follow-up, and a system that turns interest into conversations.

Do I need to run paid ads?

Not always. Paid ads can create attention faster, but we can also support organic traffic, direct outreach, local search, referrals, or social media depending on your business and budget.

Can you still fix my current website?

Yes. If your website is blocking leads, we can clean up the pages, calls to action, forms, and booking flow as part of the lead system.

What does the free website check include?

Send your business name, website, and main goal. We will review your offer, website, lead capture, and follow-up, then send back the easiest opportunities to improve booked appointments.

Can AI actually book appointments?

AI can help reply quickly, qualify the lead, answer common questions, and send the right booking link. Your team still stays in control of the sales conversation and final customer experience.

What businesses are the best fit?

The best fit is usually a local service business with real demand, repeat customers, and enough revenue to invest in growth, like med spas, clinics, contractors, gyms, salons, auto shops, and home services.
